About Edith

Specifications

Edith is a next-generation AI assistant built on a powerful, multimodal large language model. It's designed for seamless integration and high-performance data processing, capable of handling complex queries with remarkable speed and accuracy.

Features

  • Advanced natural language understanding and generation.
  • Multilingual support for global accessibility.
  • Real-time data analysis and visualization tools.

Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Conversations are anonymized and not used for training purposes without explicit consent. You have full control over your data and chat history, with options to delete it at any time.
